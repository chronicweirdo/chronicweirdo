Communication breakdown and entropy

2013.04.10 12.58.00

The breakdown of communication through successive adoption of simpler and less expressive forms, like "sharing", "liking", "+1ing" and "retweeting", is inevitable since these simpler forms have a greater entropy than articulate messages (they require less energy to be maintained) and, according to the second law of thermodynamics, they are more likely to occur.
